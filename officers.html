President

Max Vorcheimer

Max Vorcheimer is honored and excited to serve as the President of the Wayne Valley FBLA chapter for the 2015-2016 school year. His role is to oversee all chapter activities, and is eager to see the fantastic things the entire board will accomplish this year. Having attended the FBLA National Conference in Chicago this past summer, Max has a strong sense of the true meaning of FBLA from a national perspective, seeing how impactful the networks made in the high school years can be for future business ventures. Max aims to have wide chapter involvement this year, hoping for a strong showing at Regional and State competitions, and hopes that even those who may not necessarily be interested in a career in business take advantage of what FBLA has to offer.
