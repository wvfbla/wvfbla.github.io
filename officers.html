Vice President

Carrington Park

Carrington Park is thrilled to be serving as a Vice President of WV FBLA this year. Her favorite thing about FBLA is the opportunity to build friendships and collaborate with people of similar interests. Her goal this year is to increase participation and help general members see their potential. She plans to have a career in the business world in the future, and thinks that FBLA networking opportunities provide her with the foundation that she needs. She believes that FBLA forms personal connections that will always benefit members in the future—business world or not.

Webmaster & Director of Professional Development

Komal Nerurkar

Komal Nerurkar is proud to bring you wvfbla.github.io, a site she created herself. She is a Goldman Sachs GWC alum and a frequent hackathon attendee who loves to participate in FBLA’s leadership conferences and chocolate sales. This year, she aims to regularly update the website and locate a competent Webmaster for next year. Komal also firmly believes that business and technology are complementary fields and hopes to be involved with both in her future career. She strongly encourages all students to get involved with FBLA and take advantage of every opportunity.
